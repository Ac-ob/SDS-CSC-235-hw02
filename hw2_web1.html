Explanatory analysis of NYC OpenData:
Urban Park Ranger Animal Condition Response

Author: Amaya McGill

Dataset information

This data set highlights calls made to the Urban Park Rangers for assistance with incidents related to animals and wildlife in New York City. The data set was provided by the Department of Parks and Recreation (DPR). Each row is a response from the Urban Park Rangers. A bias that might be present is related to the Duration of response. In the variable description they do not specify what the amount of time spent responding is recorded on (minutes, hours).


Steps for Explanatory analysis are as follow (click on the images to learn more!):

  1. Downloading the data from NYOpenData
  2. Selecting variables of interest
  3. Getting summary statistics for the variables
  4. Creating visualizations for the variables
  5. Developing hypothesis from the data
